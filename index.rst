How to Activate Tubi TV Using Code at tubi.tv/activate (2025 Guide)
========================================


1. Step 1 - Open the Tubi App on Your Smart TV
-----------------------------------------
Turn on your TV and open the Tubi app. You can use a Roku, Firestick, or any smart TV.
Once the app opens, you will see a code on the screen. Don’t worry, you will need this code in the next step. Just keep the screen open and don’t press anything.

2. step 2 - Go to tubi.tv/activate on Your Phone or Computer
-----------------------------------------
Now take your phone, tablet, or computer and open the internet browser (like Chrome or Safari).
Type tubi.tv/activate in the address bar and press Enter.
Log in to your Tubi account. If you don’t have one, click “Sign Up” — it’s free and only takes a minute!

3. Step 3 - Type the Code from Your TV
-----------------------------------------
You’ll see a box on the screen asking for the code.
Go back to your TV, look at the code, and type it exactly into the box.
Then click the button that says “Activate.” If the code is right, it will work right away.

4. Step 4 - Start Watching Tubi!
-----------------------------------------
After you click activate, your TV screen will refresh on its own.
Now you can watch free movies, TV shows, and cartoons on Tubi. No payment, no problem — just click and play!
